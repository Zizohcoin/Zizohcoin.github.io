About Zizohcoin

Zizohcoin (ZOH) is an innovative cryptocurrency aimed at building a strong and engaged community within the decentralized finance (DeFi) space. We believe in the transformative potential of cryptocurrencies and strive to democratize access to this ecosystem by actively rewarding our participants.

Project Purpose

  • Token Buyback and Distribution: We allocate 50% of the revenue generated through monetization of our website, social media, and report sales to repurchase ZOH tokens from the market. These repurchased tokens are then distributed to our community via airdrops, promoting decentralization and widespread token distribution.
  • Community Engagement: By rewarding our members with ZOH tokens, we encourage active participation and the growth of an informed and engaged community aligned with DeFi values.

Responsible Distribution Practices

Inspired by industry leaders, we emphasize the importance of ethical and transparent token distribution. Avoiding early speculation and ensuring fair token distribution are principles guiding our airdrop strategy.

We invite you to be part of Zizohcoin and contribute to building a more inclusive and informed DeFi community. By participating, you not only expand your knowledge of cryptocurrencies but also directly benefit from the collective growth and success of our project.
