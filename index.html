About Zizohcoin

Zizohcoin (ZOH) is a cryptocurrency aimed at revolutionizing the decentralized finance (DeFi) ecosystem. Its mission is to create an inclusive, engaged community where everyone can contribute to a democratic and transparent financial system.

The project focuses on fairness by using 50% of its revenue to buy back and distribute ZOH tokens to the community via airdrops. Zizohcoin promotes decentralization, active participation, and ethical distribution, ensuring long-term growth for those genuinely involved.
